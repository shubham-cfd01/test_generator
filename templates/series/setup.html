File Format Guide

Excel (.xlsx) — Use these columns:

ColumnDescriptionExample
idQuestion number1
typemcq or fill_in_the_blanksmcq
questionQuestion textWhat is 5+3?
optionsPipe-separated (MCQ only)6|7|8|9
answerCorrect answer8
diagramPaste your diagram image in this column (optional)—

Word (.docx) — Write questions in order. Everything below a question (options, pasted images, answer) belongs to that question until the next Q starts.

Tip: When pasting charts/diagrams, use Paste → Paste as Picture for best compatibility.

Q1. Find the area of the circle below.
[paste or draw your diagram here]
A) 12.56   B) 28.27   C) 50.27
Answer: 28.27
